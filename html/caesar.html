How to use

How it works: Each letter of the text to be encrypted is replaced by a letter a fixed number of positions away in the alphabet. Enter your text in the input box and choose whether to encode or decode. Set the shift key value, then click Submit to see the result. You can also break the encryption by trying all possible shifts.
